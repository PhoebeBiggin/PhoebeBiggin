My time in FoDR:

Through Foundations of Design: Representation, I was supported to refine my investigation of two and three-dimensional design outputs. Exploring work between a wide array of programs (Rhinoceros, Adobe Illustrator, Adobe Photoshop, and Adobe Indesign), Encouraging me to improve upon my skillset towards digital design problem-solving. Participating in this subject expanded my scope of creative experience to applying iterative, derivative and conceptual processes.

FoD:R incited me to consider how to approach and refine my work further through a curiosity-fuelled approach: How can I express my design concepts further?
